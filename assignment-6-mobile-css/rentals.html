Info:

Bear Creek Cabins is a family-owned and operated community with five breathtaking cabins available for rent at nightly, weekly (7-night), and monthly (30-night) rates. Each cabin has different exclusive amenities. Public amenities available to all guests are bike paths, walking trails, paved roads and driveways, an in-ground pool, high-speed internet, and access to our amazing guesthouse. Our guesthouse is open 12 PM-7 PM Monday thru Friday. The guesthouse is located right by the pool and has 3 bathrooms. A game room featuring a pool table, multiple arcade machines, and 2 TVs with PlayStations. A living room featuring an 85-inch TV, with multiple streaming services free to access and room for 14 people. Also, there is a library with over 100 books and two computers with internet access. *Guesthouse rules and regulations will be provided in your arrival information upon purchase.
